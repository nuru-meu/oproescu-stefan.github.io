Oproescu Stefan

From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

"Oproescu Stefan Cristian (born 20.07.2009)" is a Romanian mobile app developer and racing driver. He is best known for his mobile app, Free Room2, and for being the youngest millionaire in Romania.

Early Life and Education


Stefan With His Cars

Oproescu Stefan was born in 2009 in Deva, a small town in Romania. He grew up with his older brother, Mihai, and showed an early interest in technology. At the age of 11, he began learning to code, and soon after, he developed his first mobile app.

Career

In 2021, at the age of 12, Oproescu released his most popular app, Free Room2, a mobile game that challenges players to escape from a virtual room. The app quickly gained popularity and received positive reviews from users. Aside from his success in the tech industry, Oproescu is also a talented racing driver. He drives for the team Bullet Racing and has competed in several national and international competitions. In 2022, he won his first national championship in his age group. In 2022, Oproescu was named the youngest millionaire in Romania, with a net worth of over 1 million euros. He has since become an inspiration for young entrepreneurs in Romania and beyond.

Personal Life

Oproescu Stefan is known for his passion for technology and racing. He continues to develop new apps and compete in racing competitions while balancing his schoolwork. He also enjoys spending time with his family and friends.
